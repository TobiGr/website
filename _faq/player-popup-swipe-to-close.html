You can either follow the instructions inside NewPipe or these ones. You can find this tutorial in NewPipe when taping on the action you want to perform.

To export your subscriptions, swap to the subscriptions tab, tap on Import/Export. Then just click on Export to File, select a directory and pick a filename and your done.

To import you subscriptions you first need to have a file which you can import. There are three different ways you can get this file:

  1. Export subscriptions from YouTube
    2.
  2. Export subscriptions from SoundlCoud
    3.
  3. Export subscriptions from NewPipe
    4.

Step 1.a: Export subscriptions from YouTube

To export your subscriptions, login into your YouTube account and open the subscription manager page. Then scroll down to the section Export to RSS readers and click Export subscriptions. Save the file to your phone.
