Extended Privacy

NewPipe does not use the YouTube API nor the Google Play Services.


This means that we do not share any data (except the video URL) with YouTube or Google. Also, the app does not save any data from you or use services that analyse your usage behaviour.

Background Player

Use other apps with no hassle while NewPipe plays your favourite songs.


We have added a background player to NewPipe. You can do whatever you want - your music keeps playing.

And even better for you: we only download the audio, so you don't waste data volume.
